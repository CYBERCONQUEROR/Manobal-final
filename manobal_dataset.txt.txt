SECTION: FAQs
=======================================
Q: How do I deal with exam stress?  
A: Take regular breaks, practice deep breathing, and focus on one topic at a time. Avoid comparing yourself with others.  

Q: I cannot sleep before exams. What should I do?  
A: Avoid caffeine, put away screens 1 hour before bed, and try a relaxation exercise like 4-7-8 breathing.  

Q: I feel very lonely in hostel. How do I cope?  
A: Stay connected with family, engage in group activities, or join a club. Talking to a trusted peer also helps.  

Q: What should I do if I feel anxious during class?  
A: Take slow breaths, drink some water, and remind yourself it will pass. Talk to your professor or counsellor if it continues.

SECTION: COPING STRATEGIES
=======================================
Breathing Techniques:
- Box Breathing: Inhale 4 sec → Hold 4 sec → Exhale 4 sec → Hold 4 sec.  
- 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale 4 sec → Hold 7 sec → Exhale 8 sec.  

Sleep Hygiene:
- Maintain a fixed sleep/wake time.  
- Avoid caffeine or heavy meals before bed.  
- No screens 1 hour before sleeping.  

Study-Life Balance:
- Break tasks into smaller chunks.  
- Use Pomodoro technique (25 min study, 5 min break).  
- Reward yourself after completing tasks.
